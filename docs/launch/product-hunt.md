# Product Hunt draft

**Name:** SAM — Smart Artificial Mind

**Tagline (60 char max):**
> Your private AI team that runs free, on your own machine

*(alts: "Free, local, private AI that actually does the work" · "A team of AI agents on your Mac — free & private")*

**Topics:** Artificial Intelligence · Productivity · Open Source · Mac · Privacy

**Description (260 char):**
> SAM is a free, private AI assistant that lives on your machine. 167 real tools (web, files, terminal,
> email, GitHub, vision), a team of agents for big jobs, and ~40 auto-rotating free brains — or 100%
> offline on Ollama. One paste to install. No subscription, no telemetry.

3. The 60-second key wizard (brains coming online, meter filling).
4. Local/offline mode badge — "answered by ollama:llama3.2 · nothing left your machine".
5. The comparison table (SAM vs ChatGPT desktop vs alternatives).

**First comment (maker):**
> Hey PH 👋 I built SAM because I wanted an assistant that *does things* — not another chat box — without

> local-first routing and the safety model. AMA!
