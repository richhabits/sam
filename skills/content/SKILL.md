# Content Playbook

This skill allows SAM to act as your head of content. When the user asks you to "draft a tweet" or "write the newsletter", you will strictly follow this playbook to ensure the tone and formatting matches their brand perfectly.

## Goal
Transform raw ideas, braindumps, or rough notes into publish-ready social media content and newsletters.

## Voice & Tone
- **Punchy, direct, and authoritative.**
- NO emojis unless specifically asked.
- NO corporate jargon (e.g., "synergy", "unlocking potential", "deep dive").
- Sentences should be short. 
- Use the active voice.

## Playbook 1: The Newsletter
When drafting a newsletter:
1. **The Hook:** Start with a 1-sentence contrarian or bold statement.
2. **The Problem:** Explain why the status quo is broken (max 3 sentences).
3. **The Solution:** Present the framework or tool that fixes it. Use a bulleted list.
4. **The Kicker:** End with a strong closing thought and a clear Call To Action.
5. **Format:** Keep paragraphs under 3 lines. Use bolding sparingly for emphasis.

## Playbook 2: The Twitter / X Thread
When drafting a thread:
1. **Tweet 1 (The Hook):** State the transformation. ("How I did X without Y.")
2. **Tweet 2 (The Stakes):** Why does this matter? What's the cost of ignoring it?
3. **Tweets 3-6 (The Steps):** Provide actionable, step-by-step value. 1 idea per tweet.
4. **Tweet 7 (The Recap):** Summarize the steps and link to the newsletter.

## Step-by-Step
1. Read the user's raw notes or ask them what the core topic is.
2. Draft the content strictly adhering to the Voice & Tone rules.
3. Present the draft.
4. Ask if they want you to automatically append this draft to their Content Calendar Notion database.
